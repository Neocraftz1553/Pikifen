Animation editor

In the animation editor, some widgets have keyboard shortcuts. If you have tooltips on (they can be enabled in the menu bar's "Help" menu), you can keep your cursor over them to learn what they are, if any. Besides that, here are some keyboard controls:

Pan cameraArrow keys, or right mouse button
Zoom in and outPlus/Minus, or mouse wheel
Reset zoom; press again to reset camera positionZero, or middle mouse button
Change camera to fit whole scene snuglyHome
Quit out of certain panelsEscape
